Bug Bag

Organic            Natural            Hardened            Intellegent            Transparent

The Bug Bag is made of waxed hemp canvas. Its main body is 100% natural. The bag can use NFC to recognize items marked by NFC tags. When put inside, the e-paper screen will display what is inside the bag.
